Get to know me!

Hi! I'm Ivie Borlongan, a student and an artist. Please allow me to present myself as a creative artist. I have a distinct style that combines old and contemporary techniques, resulting in breathtaking and thought-provoking pieces. About Me

Who am I as a student and an artist?

Being an artist and a student can be a unique and exciting experience. Pursuing a passion for creativity while also striving for academic excellence can be challenging, but it can also be incredibly rewarding. As an artist and a student, one is constantly learning and growing in various areas of life. The dedication and discipline required for both pursuits can help in developing a well-rounded and successful individual. Whether it's through expressing oneself creatively or gaining knowledge in the classroom, being an artist and a student offers endless opportunities for growth and self-discovery.
